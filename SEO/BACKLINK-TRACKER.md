## Notes & Tips

### Best Practices
1. **Personalize Every Email:** Mention specific articles or content from their site
2. **Provide Value First:** Don't just ask for links, offer something useful
3. **Follow Up:** 50% of responses come after follow-ups
4. **Track Everything:** Use this spreadsheet religiously
5. **Build Relationships:** Think long-term, not just one-time links
